SUFFRAGETTES

is a collaborative experience that celebrates
New Zealand women's suffrage

Celebrating 125 years of women's suffrage

Suffragettes is a project that commemorates the women who fought for the right to vote. It encourages people all over New Zealand to participate, showing their support today. Users scan their New Zealand ten dollar note. The notes serial number is matched with a 1893 suffragette. The suffragette is then mapped where the petition was signed.
